Purpose

Sometimes making appointments can be a hassle, both for the business and for the customer.
Thus, keeping track of all the appointments eats away at time that could be spent doing more important work.
With Appointment Hub, the goal is to provide businesses with a way to manage appointments for customers,
as well as allow customers to schedule appointments virtually with an easy to access platform and user interface.
